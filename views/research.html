what I work on

I develop robust, ML-powered software systems combining statistical modeling and signal processing. I design and build scalable data pipelines, simulations, and embedded applications that process complex, noisy real-world data—from biological signals to wireless networks—focusing on reliability, interpretability, and efficiency in resource-constrained environments.

🛠️ Simulating Wireless Congestion & QoS Degradation

Built an end-to-end simulation pipeline for wireless network congestion using MATLAB, Simulink, WLAN Toolbox, and Communications Toolbox. Modeled multi-user traffic and congestion scenarios on IEEE 802.11-based WLAN networks. Analyzed latency, throughput, and packet loss across user loads and identified critical thresholds where performance degraded. Proposed CSMA/CA protocol optimizations to enhance QoS. Focused on real-time simulation for embedded and IoT systems.

⌚ Nonparametric ML for HR Prediction

Designed a contactless heart rate prediction system using only Apple Watch tri-axial motion accelerometer data. Applied a nonparametric copula-based model to capture nonlinear dependencies between motion and heart rate during sleep. Personalized predictions that outperformed baseline in 20 of 26 subjects. Paper presented and accepted at IEEE EMBC Copenhagen (peer-reviewed) and CPH Frontiers @ UCSF–UC Berkeley.

🛥️ Vessel Trajectory Forecasting with NanoGPT & GeoEncoding

Developed a forecasting system for predicting vessel movements in maritime fisheries using GeoHash encoding + NanoGPT. Transformed AIS data into integer sequences, enabling low-memory transformer training in constrained environments. Tackled vocab explosion, sparsity, and convergence via token design and regularization. Focused on fixed-gear vessels. Presented at the CDSS Discovery Symposium with InferLink Corp..

🧬 CNN-Based Detection of Macular Degeneration

Developed a CNN to detect age-related macular degeneration from retinal images. Implemented preprocessing with normalization, augmentation, and artifact removal for robustness. Leveraged transfer learning (ResNet, EfficientNet), and addressed class imbalance using weighted loss and oversampling. Collaborated with University of Cambridge clinicians to ensure clinical relevance and interpretability. Built using TensorFlow/Keras and OpenCV.

Read the paper on arXiv

♟️ RCT: Chess-Based Cognitive Training in Rural Africa

Analyzed longitudinal data from a World Bank–funded randomized controlled trial spanning 15 primary schools in Kenya. Assessed intervention effects on working memory, executive function, and non-cognitive skills using robust linear models and Difference-in-Differences (DiD) regression to control for confounders and time trends. Research conducted in collaboration with UC Berkeley Economics.

🌙 Detecting Sleep-Wake Cycles & Circadian Instability

Building a signal processing pipeline to detect circadian rhythm disruption from actigraphy data. Extracting rest–activity patterns using Fourier analysis, smoothing, and instability scoring. This work aims to enable passive, low-burden monitoring of neurological health from modern consumer wearable sensors.
