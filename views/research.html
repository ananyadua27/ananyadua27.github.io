what I work on

I develop robust, ML-powered software systems that combine statistical modeling and signal processing. I design and build scalable data pipelines, simulations, and embedded applications that process complex, noisy real-world data—from biological signals to wireless networks—focusing on reliability, interpretability, and efficiency in resource-constrained environments.

🛠️ Simulating Wireless Congestion & QoS Degradation

Built an end-to-end simulation pipeline for wireless network congestion using MATLAB, Simulink, WLAN Toolbox, and Communications Toolbox. Modeled multi-user traffic and congestion scenarios on IEEE 802.11-based WLAN networks. Analyzed latency, throughput, and packet loss across user loads and identified critical thresholds where performance degraded. Proposed CSMA/CA protocol optimizations to enhance QoS. Focused on real-time simulation for embedded and IoT systems.

⌚ Nonparametric ML for HR Prediction

Designed a contactless heart rate prediction system using only Apple Watch tri-axial motion accelerometer data. Applied a nonparametric copula-based model to capture nonlinear dependencies between motion and heart rate during sleep. Personalized predictions that outperformed baseline in 20 of 26 subjects. Paper accepted and presented at IEEE EMBC Copenhagen (peer-reviewed) and CPH Frontiers @ UCSF–UC Berkeley.

🛥️ Vessel Trajectory Forecasting with NanoGPT & GeoEncoding

Developed a forecasting system for predicting vessel movements in maritime fisheries using GeoHash encoding + NanoGPT. Transformed AIS data into integer sequences, enabling low-memory transformer training in constrained environments. Tackled vocab explosion, sparsity, and convergence via token design and regularization. Focused on fixed-gear vessels. Presented at the CDSS Discovery Symposium with InferLink Corp..

🗳️ Bluebonnet Data: Distinguishing Young Voters in Watauga County

Supported Ben Massey's NC House campaign by integrating Appalachian State registrar data, VAN files, and public state voter records using SQL. Employed fuzzy matching and automated PDF-to-CSV extraction to bypass VAN export limits. Developed a Tableau dashboard with ArcGIS mapping to visualize and enable targeted student voter outreach.

🧬 CNN-Based Detection of Macular Degeneration

Developed a CNN to detect age-related macular degeneration from retinal images. Implemented preprocessing with normalization, augmentation, and artifact removal for robustness. Leveraged transfer learning (ResNet, EfficientNet), and addressed class imbalance using weighted loss and oversampling. Collaborated with University of Cambridge clinicians to ensure clinical relevance and interpretability. Built using TensorFlow/Keras and OpenCV.

🌍 Impact Evaluations - World Bank & UC Berkeley Economics

Assessed the cognitive and non-cognitive effects of a youth chess intervention in rural schools in Kenya using difference-in-differences regression analysis, controlling for time and group fixed effects. Second, evaluated Brazil’s "Minha Casa, Minha Vida" housing program by integrating a geolocation API to delineate precise geographic boundaries, enabling spatially-informed comparisons of pre- and post-intervention economic indicators at the community level.
